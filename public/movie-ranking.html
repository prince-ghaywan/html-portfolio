The Best movies according to Prince

Top 3 movies of All-Time

Doctor Strange

Doctor Strange is a Marvel superhero film about Dr. Stephen Strange, a brilliant but arrogant neurosurgeon whose career ends after a car accident damages his hands. In search of healing, he discovers the mystic arts and trains under the Ancient One. He learns to harness magic, travel between dimensions, and protect Earth from dark mystical forces — ultimately becoming the Sorcerer Supreme.

Avengers

The Avengers series is a four-movie Marvel saga where Earth’s mightiest heroes — including Iron Man, Captain America, Thor, Hulk, Black Widow, and Hawkeye — unite to face threats too great for any one hero. Across the films, they battle global dangers like Ultron and cosmic enemies like Thanos, culminating in the fight to protect the universe and restore balance.

Harry Potter

Harry Potter is a fantasy film series based on J.K. Rowling’s novels. It follows a young wizard, Harry Potter, who discovers on his 11th birthday that he is a wizard and attends Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Along with his friends Hermione Granger and Ron Weasley, he faces magical adventures while uncovering the truth about his past and battling the dark wizard Lord Voldemort, who seeks immortality and control over the wizarding world.
